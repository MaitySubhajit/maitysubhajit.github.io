Research Interests

My research focus is broadly centered around Deep Learning and its applications in Computer Vision problems. In the past few years I have explored diverse range of topics including Self-supervised Learning, Object Detection, Document Layout Analysis, Knowledge Distillation, Image Retrieval, Scene Text Recognition. Currently, I am looking for PhD opportunities and am highly interested to collaborate on Computer Vision and Machine Learning based research projects.
