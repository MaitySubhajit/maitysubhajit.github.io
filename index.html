Research Interests

My research is broadly focused on the intersection of Machine Learning and Computer Vision. In the past few years, I have explored a diverse range of topics including Self-supervised Learning, Object Detection, Document Layout Analysis, Knowledge Distillation, Image Retrieval, Scene Text Recognition, Vision Transformers, Few-Shot Learning, Keypoint Detection. Currently, I am working on Large Multimodal Models, Kolmogorov Arnold Representations, Attention Mechanism at UCF.
